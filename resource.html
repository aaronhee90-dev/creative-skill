THE MATERIALS

The majority of us began drawing in our early years. Materials and tools didn't really matter to us back then. Maybe they weren't even taken into account. Often, a regular pencil and some notebook paper would "do the trick." However, the materials and tools we selected became increasingly crucial as we matured as artists. As the relationship between high-quality art supplies and high-quality artwork becomes apparent, it is understandable why up-and-coming artists want the greatest materials available.
